About

HacktivCASH is obsessed with creating and sharing unique and authentic product experiences that stimulate our fans' imaginations and fuel their geek core. Since 1999, HacktivCASH has been on a mission to create a world where everyone can embrace their inner geek and connect with one another. What started as a place for clever t-shirts and unusual gifts is now the best way to find wondrous goods that express all your passions - much of which you won't find anywhere else on Earth (or any other planet). Shop for apparel, functional and fun home and office decor, electronics and gadgets, outdoor and survival gear, collectibles, and much more. Find the perfect gift, whether your loved one is into science or science fiction. Get things you need and things you NEED (pronounced: want). Or just prank your office mate.
